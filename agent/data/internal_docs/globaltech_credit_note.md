# GlobalTech Industries — Credit Committee Note

**Analyst:** S. Chen | **Date:** August 22, 2024 | **Rating:** BB- (Positive)

## Business Overview
GlobalTech Industries is an enterprise software and IT services company serving mid-market businesses across North America and Europe. The company provides cloud-based ERP solutions, managed IT services, and cybersecurity platforms. ~6,800 employees, HQ in Austin, TX.

## Financial Summary (LTM Q2 2024)
- Revenue: $2.4B (up 12% YoY, organic growth 8%)
- EBITDA: $576M (margin: 24.0%)
- ARR: $1.6B (recurring revenue 67% of total)
- Total Debt: $1,300M (Term Loan: $800M, CLO: $500M)
- Net Leverage: 2.1x (down from 2.5x post-acquisition)
- Interest Coverage: 4.5x
- Free Cash Flow: $210M
- Net Revenue Retention: 112%

## Key Strengths
- High recurring revenue base provides cash flow visibility
- Strong net retention indicates product stickiness and upsell capacity
- Cybersecurity segment growing 25% YoY — secular tailwind
- Demonstrated ability to integrate bolt-on acquisitions (3 completed since 2022)

## Key Risks
- Integration risk from recent Dataflow Systems acquisition ($180M, closed Q1 2024)
- Margin pressure from enterprise sales cycle lengthening (pipeline +15% but close rates down 5%)
- Key-person risk: CTO departure in Q1 2024, replacement still ramping
- European segment (~20% revenue) exposed to FX headwinds

## Outlook
Credit profile improving. Recurring revenue growth and deleveraging support a potential upgrade path. We are monitoring the Dataflow integration and CTO transition closely. Recommend maintaining current exposure with potential to increase on next refi.
