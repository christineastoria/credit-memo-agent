# Acme Corp — Q3 2024 Credit Review

**Analyst:** J. Morrison | **Date:** October 15, 2024 | **Rating:** BB- (Stable)

## Business Overview
Acme Corp is a diversified industrials manufacturer specializing in precision-engineered components for aerospace, automotive, and heavy machinery end markets. Founded in 1987, headquartered in Cleveland, OH. ~4,200 employees across 12 manufacturing facilities in the US and Mexico.

## Financial Summary (LTM Q3 2024)
- Revenue: $1.85B (up 6% YoY)
- EBITDA: $370M (margin: 20.0%)
- Total Debt: $950M (Term Loan B: $550M, Senior Notes: $400M)
- Net Leverage: 2.4x (down from 2.7x at close)
- Interest Coverage: 3.8x
- Free Cash Flow: $145M (FCF yield: 15.3%)
- Capex: $85M (4.6% of revenue)

## Key Strengths
- Strong market position in aerospace components (25% market share in precision fasteners)
- Diversified end-market exposure reduces cyclicality
- Consistent deleveraging trajectory since 2022 LBO
- Management team has 15+ years average tenure

## Key Risks
- Automotive segment (~30% of revenue) exposed to OEM production cuts
- Top 5 customers represent 42% of revenue — moderate concentration
- Raw material costs (steel, titanium) subject to commodity price swings
- Union labor at 3 of 12 facilities — next CBA negotiation in Q2 2025

## Recommendation
Maintain current exposure. Credit trajectory is positive with steady deleveraging. Monitor automotive segment performance and upcoming union negotiations. No change to internal risk rating (2).
